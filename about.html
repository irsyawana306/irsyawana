About UiTM E-Profile

UiTM E-Profile is a platform designed to streamline the management of student data, research projects, and academic progress. It allows both students and faculty to interact with student profiles and academic journeys at UiTM.

VISION

To create an integrated, sustainable platform that fosters academic and research excellence for UiTM students and faculty.

MISSION

To provide a seamless, interactive platform that supports academic development, research collaboration, and efficient data management for students and faculty.

Key Features

Personalized Profiles

Students can manage their own academic profiles, track achievements, and share progress with faculty.

Research Tracking

Track ongoing research projects, collaborate with peers, and monitor academic progress.

Secure Data Management

Ensure the safety and privacy of academic and research data within a secure and encrypted environment.
